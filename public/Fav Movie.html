The Best Movies in my opinion

My top 3 movies of All-Time

The Nun

When a young nun at a cloistered abbey in Romania takes her own life, a priest with a haunted past and a novitiate on the threshold of her final vows are sent by the Vatican to investigate.

Three Steps Above Heaven

Based on a novel by Federico Moccia, the film tells the story of Babi and Hache, a wealthy girl and a rebellious boy who fall in love despite their social differences. The title refers to the feeling of being close to the sky when you are in love.

The Forge

Isaiah Wright has some growing up to do. A year out of high school with no plans for his future, Isaiah is challenged by his single mom
